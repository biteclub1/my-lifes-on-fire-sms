Privacy Policy

Effective July 3, 2026

Information we collect

When you join the My Life’s on Fire comedy-show waitlist, we may collect your name, mobile telephone number, your text-message responses, opt-in and opt-out records, and reservation-related information.

How we use information

We use this information to manage the comedy-show waitlist, send reservation announcements, confirm attendance, provide show updates, answer support requests, and maintain records of consent and opt-out requests.

Mobile information and sharing

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the messaging program, process reservations, or comply with law. Those providers may use the information only to perform services for us.

SMS disclosures

Message frequency varies based on show scheduling and reservation activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchase.

Data retention and security

We retain information only as reasonably necessary to operate the waitlist, document consent, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards, but no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure.

Your choices

You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us at abuschickenandfish@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
