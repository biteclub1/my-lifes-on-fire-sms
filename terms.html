Program description

The My Life’s on Fire SMS program provides comedy-show waitlist confirmations, reservation announcements, attendance reminders, schedule or venue updates, and related customer support.

Opt-in

You may opt in by texting FIRE to (702) 475-8779 and replying with your first and last name when prompted. By completing the opt-in flow, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages from My Life’s on Fire at the mobile number you provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message frequency and charges

Message frequency varies depending on show scheduling and reservation activity. Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier’s terms also apply.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP to unsubscribe. After opting out, you may receive one confirmation message and then no further program messages unless you opt in again. Reply HELP for help or email abuschickenandfish@gmail.com.

Availability

Delivery of text messages is subject to effective transmission by your mobile carrier and is not guaranteed. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. We may change or discontinue the program at any time.
